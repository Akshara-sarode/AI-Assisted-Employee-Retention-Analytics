# 🤖 AI-Assisted Employee Retention Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview

AI-Assisted Employee Retention Analytics is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze workforce trends, employee demographics, attrition patterns, and job satisfaction levels. The dashboard transforms raw HR data into actionable insights, enabling HR professionals and business leaders to make data-driven decisions that improve employee retention, workforce planning, and overall organizational performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Monitor overall workforce statistics
- Analyze employee attrition across departments and salary slabs
- Understand workforce demographics such as age and gender
- Evaluate job satisfaction across different roles
- Identify trends affecting employee retention
- Support strategic HR decision-making through analytics

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Attrition by Department
Visualizes employee turnover across departments to identify areas with higher attrition rates.

### 2️⃣ Attrition by Salary Slab
Analyzes attrition patterns across salary ranges to uncover compensation-related retention challenges.

### 3️⃣ Job Role & Job Satisfaction Analysis
Evaluates satisfaction levels across different job roles to understand workforce engagement.

### 4️⃣ Age Group Distribution
Displays workforce demographics across various age groups.

### 5️⃣ Attrition by Gender
Compares attrition trends between male and female employees.

### 6️⃣ Attrition Trend by Experience
Shows how years of experience influence employee turnover.

### 7️⃣ Department-wise Employee Count
Provides insights into workforce distribution across departments.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- The organization experiences an overall attrition rate of **16.3%**
- Administration and Sales departments contribute significantly to employee turnover
- Employees aged **26–35 years** form the largest workforce segment
- Attrition is higher among employees with lower experience levels
- Male employees account for a larger share of attrition compared to female employees
- Operations is the largest department by workforce size
